<div class="col">
<p>Hello! I'm Sydney "Syd" Horan. I am a biologist who has worked on host-parasite relationships in several systems, from newts to nestlings. I am currently a <b>Ph.D. candidate</b> at the University of Connecticut in the <a href="https://eeb.uconn.edu/">Department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology</a>, with an estimated graduation in Spring 2027.</p><p> My overall interest is the influence of environmental change and human influence on host-parasite (and host-pathogen) systems. I employ a mixture of field studies, laboratory work, and natural history collections to answer these questions. I also love <a href="/outreach">outreach</a> and the ability to integrate my creative passions, such as art and photography, with my work to better communicate ideas. You'll see my own photography and art featured in many of my presentations!</p>
